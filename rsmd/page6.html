In 2009, 2010 and most recently in 2013, Dr. Silich was listed as one of New York Magazine’s Best Doctors. Dr. Silich was the youngest plastic surgeon to be placed on this list in 2006. He is also listed in the Castle Connolly Top Doctors Guide. He has appeared on CNN and his work has been recognized in The New York Times, The New York Post, The Washington Post, The New York Daily News, Elle Magazine, W magazine, Quest magazine, Hamptons and Gotham magazine, and was featured in New York Magazine as one of New York City’s Cutting Edge plastic surgeons in 2003. In 2004, Dr. Silich was featured in W magazine as one of the country’s foremost up and coming plastic surgeons.
